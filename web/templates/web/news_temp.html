Explainable Artificial Intelligence Center (August 2017)

SAIL@UNIST hosted one of the next generation Artificial Intelligence Center. The center will investigate models, algorithms and systems for explainable artificial intelligence.

This project is joint effort with KAIST, Korea university, Yonsei Severance Hospitial and AItrics.

2017 Rotman-UNIST Trading Competition (August 2017)

On August 10, UNIST hosted the first Rotman-UNIST Trading Competition (RUTC), bringing together teams of primarily graduate-level students and their faculty advisors from top-ranked universities across South Korea to partake in a unique two-day simulated market challenge.

This project is doing as a workshop for XAICnd AItrics.
